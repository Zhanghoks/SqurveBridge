# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

We pledge to make participation in the SqurveBridge community harassment-free
and welcoming. Be respectful, accept constructive feedback, take responsibility
for mistakes, and focus on the health of the project. Harassment, trolling,
personal attacks, and publishing private information are unacceptable.

Maintainers are responsible for enforcing these standards. Report incidents
privately through the contact route in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

This text is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1:
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html>.
